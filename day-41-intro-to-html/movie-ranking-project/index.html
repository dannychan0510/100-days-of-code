My Favourite Movies of All Time

In no particular order

The Martian

Amazing sci-fic movie, so positive. Full of hope and positivity, and demonstrates the resilience of the human spirit. Also love the "Love Train" song choice at the end of the movie!

The Lord of the Rings Trilogy

Masterpiece. Enough said. Also, please do yourselves a favour and watch the Extended Editions!

The Wolf of Wall Street

So much greed and debauchery. Which is also what makes it infitely rewatchable!

Denis Villeneuve's Dune

Arguably an instant masterpiece, one for the ages. Denis is a genius.

Your Name

The movie that put Shinkai Makoto's name on the global radar. Arguably his best movie (as of 2023, IMO).

A Silent Voice

Also known as "The Shape of Sound". Personally this touched me alot.
